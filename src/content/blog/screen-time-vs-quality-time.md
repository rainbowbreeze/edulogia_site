# Screen Time vs. Quality Time

Not all screen time is created equal. As parents, it is easy to feel overwhelmed by the sheer volume of digital content available to our children. However, by distinguishing between passive consumption and active, educational technology use, we can foster healthier relationships with the digital world.

## Passive vs. Active Consumption

Passive consumption includes activities like mindlessly scrolling through social media or watching endless auto-playing videos. Active consumption, on the other hand, involves creation, interaction, and mindful engagement.

* Creating digital art
* Learning to code
* Composing music
* Fact-checking and research

These activities turn technology from a consumption device into a creative tool.
