# Minecraft as a Learning Tool

Sandbox games like Minecraft have changed the educational landscape. They are unparalleled in their ability to teach complex spatial reasoning, architecture, and logic.

## Redstone and Logic Gating

Redstone circuits function similarly to real-world electrical wiring and logic gates (AND, OR, NOT). Children intuitively learn binary logic while building automated farms or piston doors.

## Collaborative Problem Solving

When children share a server, they must negotiate resource allocation, plan massive builds, and establish rules for their digital society. It is a sandbox for civic engagement.
