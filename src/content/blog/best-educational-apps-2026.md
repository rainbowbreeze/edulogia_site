# Top 10 Educational Apps

Finding good apps is hard. We have reviewed the latest applications that promote genuine learning without manipulative reward loops or intrusive advertising.

1.  **Scratch**: Visual block programming.
2.  **Duolingo ABC**: Early literacy.
3.  **Swift Playgrounds**: Learning to code in Swift.
4.  **Khan Academy Kids**: Comprehensive early learning.
5.  **Toca Builders**: Open-ended creativity and building.
6.  **Endless Alphabet**: Vocabulary building with animations.
7.  **Sago Mini World**: Safe, exploratory play.
8.  **DragonBox Algebra**: Making math intuitive.
9.  **Procreate Dreams**: Accessible animation.
10. **Bookful**: Augmented reality reading.
